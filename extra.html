About Us.

We are a pair of students in the Monterrey Institue of Technology, currently rolled in the Web Applications Development curse.

was to ease the interactivity gap between individuals searching to trade physical cards of Magic the Gadering. As it has been proven
to be a rather dificutl experience, we hope to achieve with this application an easier user experience in that matter.
